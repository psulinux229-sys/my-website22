A ball is thrown horizontally from the top of a 20m high building with an initial velocity of 15 m/s. How long does it take for the ball to hit the ground?
(Assume g = 9.8 m/s² and ignore air resistance)

lightbulb

Quick Explanation

Use the vertical motion equation: h = ½gt². Solving for t gives √(2h/g). √(40/9.8) ≈ 2.02s. The horizontal velocity doesn't affect the time to fall.
